The Biology Department

Welcome to the Biology department! We are dedicated to providing the most advanced laboratory resources, as well as the proper instruction to utilize them. Our courses teach SUNY Gold students how to succeed in ecosystem studies. We offer a wide range of courses, as well as a major in Biology!

Academic Coursework

The following Biology courses are offered at SUNY Gold:

BIO101: Introduction to Biology

BIO105: Biology for Non-Majors

BIO130: Basic Biology 1

BIO150: Basic Biology 2

BIO300: Molecular Biology

BIO350: Animal Biology

BIO500: Senior Thesis
